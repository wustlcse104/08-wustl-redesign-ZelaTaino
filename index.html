Grace Hopper Conference 2018

Come learn about GHC18, the WiCS scholarship, and listen to GHC17 attendees talk about their experience with interviews/internships/full-time jobs. They'll also share useful preparation tips for you to better prepared for GHC18.

Amazon Reverse Career Fair

Students host a table and Amazon recruiters visit tables to learn more about students. Use this as an opportunity to learn more about jobs and internship opportunities relevant to you and talk to employers about how they can partner with you.

WiCS has a rule that you must apply to at least 3 outside scholarships to be considered for Wash U WiCS GHC scholarship. Here is an opportunity to check one of the three off your list.
